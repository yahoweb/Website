03 — Branding · Web

HV.Inc

Online hate reframed as a contagious social virus — a speculative brand that exposes digital toxicity by giving it a corporate identity.

Type Branding · Web
Year 2025
Role Brand + Web Designer
Client HV.Inc (Fictional)
Scope Identity · Web · Brand System
Tools Photoshop · Illustrator · VS Code
Status Academic Project

Overview

HV.inc treats online hate as a contagious social virus. In digital networks, hostility spreads through comments, shares, and reactions — infecting communities and amplifying harm in ways that mirror biological contagion.

By visualizing these patterns through a corporate identity, the project reframes online behavior as a public health concern — advocating collective responsibility in digital spaces.

Approach

Merging a viral form with hazard symbolism, the HV.inc mark visualizes online hate as a contagious threat — calling for awareness, responsibility, and collective action. Inspired by warning systems and hazard signals, the palette of burnt orange, acid yellow, and carbon black reinforces urgency while preserving legibility.

The brand extends into a fully built website that functions as a real corporate site — product pages, testimonials, reporting infrastructure. The horror is in the mundanity of it all.
