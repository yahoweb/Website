01 — Branding

Cannes

Repositioning the world's most prestigious film festival as a cultural bridge — where cinema transcends borders and becomes a shared human language.

Type Branding
Year 2025
Role Brand Designer
Client Cannes Film Festival
Scope Identity · Print · Motion
Tools Photoshop · Illustrator
Status Academic Project

Overview

The Cannes Film Festival is globally recognized, yet its identity had grown static — a relic of European prestige that failed to reflect the increasingly global nature of cinema itself.

This rebrand explores a new visual language rooted in the idea of cinema as cultural bridge: a dynamic system built on contrast, dialogue, and the tension between the familiar and the foreign.

Approach

The wordmark balances a classical serif with stark geometric cuts — echoing the duality of tradition and the avant-garde that the festival celebrates. The deep crimson palette draws from the iconic red carpet while grounding the identity in something more cinematic and intimate.

A modular grid system allows the identity to adapt across posters, credentials, digital platforms, and motion — maintaining coherence at every touchpoint.
